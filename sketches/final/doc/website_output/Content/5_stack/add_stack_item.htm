Adding an item to a stack

At the bottom of the Inspector pane, click Add This Item to Your Stack.

Haystacks adds the item currently displayed in the Inspector to your stack and displays a colored bar in the pane below the Inspector:

  • The color of the bar represents the color of the item's main subject class.
  • The height of the bar models the item's height.
  • The width of the bar models the number of pages that the item contains.
