Subject bar

The Subject bar appears on the left of a search-results graph. The Subject bar is the vertical (y) axis of the search results.

The various colored sections of the Subject bar and the corresponding labels indicate the top-level LC class numbers for items in the search results. The vertical position of a dot on the graph and the dot's color indicates the main LC class of the item's call number.
